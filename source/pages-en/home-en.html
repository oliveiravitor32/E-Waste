Eletronic waste

The electronic waste by the name itself it is already possible to notice that it comes from electronic materials, being of inorganic origin some examples of it are: copper, lead, aluminum...

In addition, the electronic waste can implicate the lives of beings and the environment since they are composed of very polluting elements compromising the environment and also when these elements come into contact with the skin can lead to various diseases for animals and humans.
