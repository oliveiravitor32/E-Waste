What is electronic waste?

E-waste is all waste from electronic materials, being of inorganic origin some examples of it are: copper, lead, aluminum...

With the exponential evolution of technology, the use of electronic devices is growing every day. more and more, and year after year companies launch more and more electronic gadgets inserting their need to improve people's lives, who often end up buying state-of-the-art equipment, often without such a need and consequently generating more and more junk mail.

Therefore, it is necessary to make people aware that electronic waste can be harmful. the lives of beings and the environment, as they are composed of very polluting elements compromising the environment and also many of these elements in contact with the skin can bring various diseases to animals and humans.
